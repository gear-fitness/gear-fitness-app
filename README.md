## Overview

Staying consistent at the gym is harder than it needs to be. Lifters waste time digging through old logs to find last week's numbers, struggle to know when they're ready to add weight or reps, and rarely get a clear picture of their progress over time. Gear Fitness fixes that by pairing a fast, friction-free workout logger with a social feed that keeps you accountable.

- **Fast logging.** Track sets, reps, and weights mid-set without fighting the interface.
- **History at a glance.** Pull up your last numbers for any exercise with a single tap.
- **Progress you can see.** Streaks, personal records, and per-exercise trends keep momentum visible.
- **A community to lift with.** Share workouts, follow friends, and stay motivated together.
- **Built-in guidance.** An AI assistant answers form and execution questions on demand.

## Features

- 🏋️ **Workout Logging** — Log workouts set-by-set with reps, weight, and automatic PR detection
- 📝 **Reusable Routines** — Save templates and schedule them to specific days of the week
- 🔍 **Exercise Library** — Search an extensive database or create your own custom exercises
- 📊 **Progress Tracking** — Review workout history, streaks, and per-exercise trends over time
- 🤝 **Social Feed** — Post completed workouts, follow others, and like and comment on posts
- 🔔 **Push Notifications** — Stay in the loop on likes, comments, and follows
